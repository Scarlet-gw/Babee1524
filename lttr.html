A Letter For You

People often say long-distance relationships do not work, but I do not believe that. Hearts pick people not places.
And it hurts that your place happens to be 242 miles away, but that does not lessen my feelings for you.
And yes, it is hard. I have never missed someone so profoundly without actually losing them,
and missing you is one of the hardest things I have ever endured.
I see other couples enjoying simple moments— goodbye kisses, falling asleep to the rhythm of a heartbeat close by—
instead of our whispered goodnights over the phone.
But it is all worth it the moment I run into your arms and feel at home again.
Being apart drives me crazy, but not being yours at all would be simply far worse.
So, I will keep fighting and loving you with everything I have,
because crossing state lines and enduring long train rides are small sacrifices
for the forever I can not wait to share with you.

Made for Soumyajit Dutta
By Scarlet (Shreya)
